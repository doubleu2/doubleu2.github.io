Services

We provide tuition to children from age 4-11. Children work on a number of activities, where they cover a range of subject areas and utilise different methods of learning. It is crucial that success is achieved and recognised even when a child is challenged as this helps develop confidence and self-esteem.

Ages and Subjects

We provide teaching for all Primary ages – Reception to Year 6

Early Years Foundation Stage – ages 4 -5

Key Stage 1 – ages 5-7

Key Stage 2 – ages 7-11

We cover a range of subjects and topic areas:

  • Phonics
  • Reading
  • English
  • Handwriting
  • Maths with Maths Mastery

Exam Preparation:

  • Year 2 SATs
  • Year 6 SATs
  • 11+ Entrance exams and interviews (Grammar and Private Schools- HABS, QE BOYS) Covering; Maths, English, Verbal Reasoning and Non-Verbal Reasoning

Learning Groups

Classes are weekly, and children are taught in small groups (of up to 4 children). Classes are arranged so all children in a particular class are at a similar level and following similar specifications. These classes have all the advantages of an interactive environment - but are effective enough for children to receive plenty of individual attention. From our years of experience we have found they work very well and have proved extremely beneficial to many children’s who have made good progress.
